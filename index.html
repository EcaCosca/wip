About Us

Unleash your creative potential at Worspace In Progress, Poblenou's newest coworking launchpad. Founded in January 2024, WIP is more than just a workspace - it's a vibrant community of creators bathed in natural light and surrounded by Poblenou's architectural gems. Here, you'll collaborate with like-minded individuals, fueled by the electric energy of the neighborhood and inspired by WIP's unique design. Whether you're a coder, painter, or entrepreneur, WIP will be your creative sanctuary.
